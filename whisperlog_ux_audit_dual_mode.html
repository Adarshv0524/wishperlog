Design principles
Six laws of WhisperLog UX
01
Capture beats cognition drift
Tap to voice or type. No navigation, no modes, no modals before the thought lands.
02
Local save is the contract
Confirmation fires on SQLite commit. Cloud and AI are invisible enrichments.
03
Motion is state, not decoration
Every animation signals a status change. Nothing moves without meaning.
04
Glass breathes depth
Four opacity levels. Hierarchy is felt through translucency, not shadow.
05
Category colours leak
In a folder, the glass surface tints to that folder's colour ramp. Felt, not seen.
06
No bottom nav. Ever.
Navigation lives at surface level. The canvas is always present.

Design innovations
Ten ideas beyond the current build
Visual systems
Kinetic folder sizing
Cards grow up to +12dp proportional to note count. Data-ink: presence = volume.
Aurora AI border
pendingAi notes get a shimmer border that dissolves on classification. Darker in light mode.
Priority halo
High priority: 6dp rose glow ring inside border. Medium: amber micro-dot only.
Time-aware mesh
Both modes shift hue 8° across the day. Light mode is cooler at dawn, warmer at dusk.
Waveform in notch
Live animated waveform inside the Notch during voice capture. Blue in dark, deeper blue in light.
Interaction patterns
Canvas momentum
Canvas grows 30–45vh as text fills, folder grid compresses below elastically.
Swipe-to-folder
Left-swipe = archive. Right-swipe = category re-assign picker (glass chip row).
Hold-home shortcut
Long-press canvas immediately activates mic — gesture parity with overlay hold.
Streak journal badge
Journal shows a flame count badge when streak ≥ 3 days. Pulses on day-open.
Pending AI count in notch
Idle notch shows a purple dot + queue count when notes are waiting for classification.
